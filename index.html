Our Core Services

Pipe Repair Works

Renopipe Liner (DN 45–2500) for common pipes; IBB16 hybrid system (DN 150–2000, up to 16 bar) for pressure pipes — covering drainage, sewage, gas & cooling mains in concrete, clay, steel & UPVC.

Confined Space Work

Skilled team trained for safe confined space operations (e.g. manholes), with full risk assessments, certified equipment, and strict precautionary protocols.

CCTV & Pipeline Survey

Pre & post-lining inspections using crawler or push-rod cameras with Pan & Tilt system; WRC-certified reports with photos & video submitted to clients.

Pipe Robotic Cutting

IMS Turbo & Pipetronic PI.CUTTER robots remove obstructions and reopen laterals in pipes DN 200–800, powered by electric, air, or hydraulic systems.

E&M & Other Works

E&M coordination & subcontracting across plumbing, drainage installation, and diverse construction projects beyond lining services.

High Pressure Jet Cleaning

300–700 bar water jetting with 360° rotating nozzle efficiently clears blockages and cleans pipe surfaces — more effective than conventional drain rods.
